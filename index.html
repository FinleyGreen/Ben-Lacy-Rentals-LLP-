Property developers and landlords

Official Details

Since 2010, Ben Lacy Rentals LLP has operated as a property developer and landlord, building experience across residential assets in the UK and Spain. The company takes a measured, long term view, improving homes so they are more comfortable, more efficient and better suited to modern living.

The work is not limited to one property type or location. Projects include period homes, family houses, rental properties and refurbishment opportunities where thoughtful investment can improve both the resident experience and the quality of the asset.

The aim is simple: create clean, well presented homes that feel cared for, function properly and stand the test of time.

Case study

24 Market Street

24 Market Street is a distinctive home in the heart of Poole Old Town, close to Poole Quay, local amenities and the historic town centre setting. The property brings together traditional external character, generous accommodation and a modern internal finish.

Project overview

PropertyCharacter residential home
LocationPoole Old Town
AccommodationFour bedrooms, two bathrooms
FocusCharacter, comfort and presentation

The property includes a modern kitchen and dining space, multiple sitting areas, a lower ground lounge, a principal bedroom with strong natural light and a fresh bathroom finish. The result is a home with a warm, individual feel while still offering the practicality expected from a well maintained rental asset.
